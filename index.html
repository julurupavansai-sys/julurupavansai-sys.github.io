Professional Overview

Dedicated Teacher, Researcher and Academic Leader

Dr. Juluru Pavanu Sai is a dedicated academic professional with strong experience in teaching, research, student mentoring, academic administration, innovation activities and institutional development.

He has actively contributed as Associate Professor, Dean IIC, Head of the Department, Research and Development Coordinator, Conference Convener, FDP Coordinator and academic event organizer.

His research interests include heat transfer, industrial heat exchangers, pulsating heat pipes, phase change materials, shell and tube heat exchanger design, biodiesel engine performance, composite materials, optimization algorithms and energy efficiency.
